KUMA // DESIGN DIRECTIONS

Five takes on the dashboard. Each one runs live on the Pi or shows sample data if you open it on your laptop. All follow DESIGN.md (mono, dark, no AI slop) and use the new Akakabuto bear. Pick one, or mix.

V1 / CONSOLE
The refined version of what is live now. CRT instrument console, green and amber signal palette, corner brackets, grouped event log. The safe, proven baseline.
 V2 / TACTICAL
Military HUD. Amber on black, reticle scope around the bear, stencil labels, coordinate grid, contacts and assessment language. Aggressive, ops-room feel.
 V3 / PHOSPHOR
Monochrome green CRT terminal. Single phosphor color, heavy scanlines and glow, command-line readout. Pip-boy energy, done with restraint.
 V4 / BRUTALIST
The outlier. Light paper background, oversized Swiss type, thick black rules, one red accent. High contrast, editorial. Bold if you want to stand apart.
 V5 / DENSE SOC
Information dense. Smaller bear, per detector tiles with live counts and sparklines, teal accent. For when you want everything on one screen at a glance.

bear-preview.html shows every bear mood at once. To run a variant live, drop the designs folder on the Pi and open it, or copy the winner into backend/kuma_api/static/dashboard.html.
